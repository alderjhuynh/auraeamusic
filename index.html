About

I'm a US based songwriter/composer. I've been playing music for a lot of my life, but piano has always been one of my favorite instruments. I typically make some sort of neoclassical and orchestral pieces but you can see me braching out into electronica, hyperpop, and artcore.

I play games like Minecraft, Silksong, and Terraria. The osts from these games have definitely influenced my compositions. I also do a little bit of coding, which you can see here from my website I handmade!

I might open commissions soon, and I'm working on a couple songs right now, I hope you enjoy them when they're out! :)
